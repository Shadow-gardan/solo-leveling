Sung Jinwoo

A special hunter who can "level up". After the Double Dungeon Incident, this former E-Rank hunter gained access to a "system" that helped him become more powerful. Though once nicknamed The Weakest Hunter of All Mankind, he now has the ability to turn the dead into his Shadow Soldiers.

Cha Hae-In

The 9th S-Rank hunter discovered in South Korea and the vice guild master of the Hunters Guild. She was the strongest hunter in South Korea before Sung Jinwoo made his debut. Due to her special ability to smell mana, she covers her nose with a handkerchief. She is fascinated to discover that Sung Jinwoo is the only hunter with a pleasant scent.

Choi Jong-In

The guild master of the Hunters Guild, the best Guild in South Korea. Nicknamed "The Ultimate Hunter," this S-Rank Mage-type hunter mainly uses Fire Magic, powerful enough to burn down a building in the blink of an eye.

Baek Yoonho

The guild master of the White Tiger Guild and an S-Rank hunter. Once a founding member of the Fiend Guild, he left to establish his own. His ability, "Beast Transformation," allows him to transform into a terrifying Magic Beast.

Yoo Jinho

The second son of Yoo Myunghan, the president of Yoojin Construction. Jinho befriended Sung Jinwoo through raids and is one of the few people Jinwoo fully trusts.

Hwang Dongsoo

Korea's 8th S-Rank Hunter who left for the US to join the Scavenger Guild. Proud of his powers, he is also the brother of C-Rank hunter Hwang Dongsuk.
